Wrote a poem to celebrate the upcoming Mid-Autumn Festival...

First, the Chinese version:

**月娥俪影洒南湾，新友笑颜溢学堂**

**望尽天涯求索路，醉邀明月伴君行**

Then, the English version:

**Angel Moon Graces South Bay, Friend's Smile Fills Study Room**

**On the Horizon Lies My Way, Come With Me Fly To the Moon**
