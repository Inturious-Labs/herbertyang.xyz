​1. If you have a super-model at your disposal, you should open your
film with a gratuitous close-up shot of your muse's almost naked thigh
and butt.

2.  If you're Michael Bay, you should just blow up everything.
3.  Decepticons always got the coolest weapons.
4.  Optimus Prime always knows how to make an entrance.

​5. If you have John Malkovich in your cast, you should let him play a
weirdo.

​6. As a human soldier, if you shoot a Decepticon's eyes, it will die or
lose combat abilities. (duh, so.... you didn't know that in T1 & T2?)

​7. As the menacing and all-mighty leader of the Decepticons who can
probably destroy a planet single-handedly with ease, you should listen
up when a lady speaks. It ain't matter that she's from the enemy's camp.
She's a smoking-hot leggy blonde. That might help though.

​8. Autobots like to kill on sight; Decepticons like to take prisoners,
and enjoy being slaughtered when those prisoners rebel.

​9. Nine Autobots can easily beat the entire army of Decepticons with
their hundreds if not thousands of combat droids.

​10. It's confirmed finally: Megatron can kill Sentinel Prime with one
fire shot; Optimus Prime can kill Megatron with one arm. You do the
math.

​11. If you stick a label on the cover lid of a white Macbook Pro, you
can call it whatever the label says, even if it's Lenovo for that
matter.

​12. The Director of National Intelligence carries around a Hermes
Birkin Ostrich Stripe Green handbag, among a few others.

​13. Autobots' fighting prowess is proportional to their physical sizes.
The same goes for Decepticons as well.

​14. Apparently, nobody likes Starscream and nobody cares where he ends
up with.
