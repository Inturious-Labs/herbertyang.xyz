The Origin of Species

For any ecosystem to strive, the parts of the system have to work well
together and create synergy. Sometimes that can be hard, because the
ecosystem may be divided into multiple sub-groups, each with its own
purpose of life, motivation, driver, sense of ownership, and resources.
If the community is trying to effectively promote at the system level
any cause, or call to action, it has to pay close attention to the
different needs of each sub-group and figure out elaborate strategies to
incentivize each group with the approach that is most appropriate and
efficient.
