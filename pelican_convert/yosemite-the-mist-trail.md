On June 22, 2013, we went to Yosemite National Park for the second time
and did the famous Mist Trail from the valley. It lived up to its
reputation - if you can afford to do only one hiking trail in all
Yosemite, this is the one. In my early days of founding Zheli.com, I
looked at EveryTrail the app when it first came out for its
location-based feature. It didn't leave me with much of an impression
back then, but now the app has fully grown into a much more mature and
feature-rich software that was actually quite useful on this trip.
