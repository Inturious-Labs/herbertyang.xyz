11. Baby I'm Amazed by You, Lonestar

I used to be on the road around the globe 100% of the time for two
years, when I was with GE's Corporate Audit Staff (CAS). It was in a bar
in mid-west America with a few CAS team members where I heard this song
for the first time. My American friends all seemed to know this song
very well. It stroke accord with all of us - that we were away from our
families and friends and we missed them.

12. Perhaps Love, John Denver

Sam and his fiancee specifically asked me to perform this particular
song on their wedding ceremony in Beijing in 2011. It is a favorite love
song of his fiancee and she always dreamed about exchanging vows with
the love of her life when this song is performed live in the background.
I knew this song fairly well - it was covered by the once-famous duo
from Taiwan, 优客李林. I compared many original versions by John Denver
and found this solo live version the best of all (even better than the
official duet version). Denver has a great voice. His singing can speak
directly to people just naturally. I wish my voice could carry that
gravitas too. Well, at least I delivered what was asked of me on the
wedding. It was a beautiful moment.

13. (I Just) Died In Your Arms Tonight, Cutting Crew

So 80s. This is one of those songs that once it hits you, it'll just
stick in your mind and never leaves.

14. With or Without You, U2

First heard about U2 from a senior in NTU, shortly after we arrived in
Singapore to start our undergraduate study there. Yizhi was also there
and he got the name right away. I had no clue at that time, yet. I was
fortunate to have the chance to watch U2 in concert when living in
Boston later. Bono was every bit as incredible as he's rumored to be.
Our rock band The Spillovers covered this song during one of the Havana
performances.
