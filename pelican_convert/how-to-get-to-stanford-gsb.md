The address of Stanford Graduate School of Business (GSB) is:

**655 Serra St, Stanford, CA**

GSB is in Knight Management Center Building, which just opened in 2011
and thus cannot be found yet on major mapping services such as Garmin,
iPhone, or Google Map. Use the above address to locate the building. The
entire area within the red-color pentagon is Knight Management Center.

[![location of Knight Management
Center](http://guizishanren.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/gsb-map.png)](http://guizishanren.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/gsb-map.png)

Once you get to the building, enter the garage from the northeast corner
of Knight Management Center, marked with a red circle. Parking is free
after 4pm. If the parking garage is full, you might be able to find some
street parking on Serra St, south of Knight Management Center.

After you get out of the garage elevator, go to the square area in the
center of Knight Management Center, surrounded by Bass Center,
McClelland Building and MBA Class of 1968 Building. It's a big open
space. You can't miss it.

There is a GSB Coupa Cafe at the east corner of this square. Voila!
