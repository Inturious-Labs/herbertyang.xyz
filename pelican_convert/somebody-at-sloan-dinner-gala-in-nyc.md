I live for moments like this. Depeche Mode's beautiful "Sombody" is one
of my favorite songs and I've always wanted to perform it live. On the
Sloan dinner night in NYC on March 28, 2013, when half-drunk Sloans took
turns on the center stage to offer their toast, I thought there would be
no better moment to do it than right here right now. So I changed a few
phrases in the original lyrics to embed some Sloan Fellow references and
off I went. A big thank-you to Anthony for recording this faithfully.

My dear friends, Sloan Fellows of 2013, I truly feel every word of this
song, from the bottom of my heart.

-------------------------------\
