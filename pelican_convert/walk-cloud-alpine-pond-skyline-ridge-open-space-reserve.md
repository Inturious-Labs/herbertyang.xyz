I really didn't expect to be able to walk in the cloud (literally) in
Palo Alto. In China you get to do this kind of cool things after
climbing 800-1000 meters on some mountains and after driving out of city
for at least two hours (if you can survive the traffic at all). It was
in the morning when the clouds were still having their morning call
meeting down in the valley (speaking of the brutal life of investment
professionals here on the West Coast). The meeting must be going well as
they didn't seem to be in a hurry to dismiss themselves.
