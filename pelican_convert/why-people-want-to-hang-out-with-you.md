It's simple. If you possess some of the following qualities, people will
want to hang out with you. Otherwise, the incentive is not there.  I
think they're collectively exhaustive and mutually exclusive.

1)  you're hot/sexy/pretty/good-looking/cute

​2) you're interesting (humorous, trendy, inspiring, cheerful,
crazy....stupid, etc)

​3) you're resourceful (money, connection, knowledge, skills,
budget....etc)

​4) you understand him/her (Note: this one is asking A LOT from you.
Very few can possess this quality with regard to anyone)

That's all there is to it, if we talk about natural, social interaction.

This should not be mistaken with the situation where people want to do
business with you. People could have a million different reasons why
they want to do business with you (like, maybe you're just phenomenal at
being ripped off).
