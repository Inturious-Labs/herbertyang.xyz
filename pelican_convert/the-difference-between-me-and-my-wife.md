My wife is widely read, so let's use this as a vehicle to explain the
difference between us.

Suppose:

=\>a book's original content                      10

=\>my wife reads it and internalizes          8

=\>my wife tells me about it                          6

=\>I comprehend what she says                  5

=\>then I tell others about the book          15

I think that pretty much explains it. We call it teamwork.
