On Wednesday's [Political
Communication](http://explorecourses.stanford.edu/search?view=catalog&filter-coursestatus-Active=on&page=0&catalog=&q=GSBGEN+565%3A+Political+Communication%3A+How+Leaders+Become+Leaders&collapse=)
class, we were divided into groups to do mocked debate representing
Winterfell and Casterly Rock. In my group I was assigned the task of
speaking about foreign policy for 1.5 minute. Before coming to the class
I only had five sentences as clueless talking points from the
perspective of Winterfell. During the class while listening to Professor
[David Demarest](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Demarest), I started
jotting down some phrases that might help me get through this 90
seconds, which accounted for 25% of the grade. I obviously didn't have
the slightest idea of Winterfell's foreign policies, but I
was reasonably versed in great speeches by Hollywood, MLK, JFK, RFK,
Yoda, and Lennon. So it became an impromptu, and seemed to have amused
my very understanding classmates, who were mostly Winterfell political
and historical junkies. Here it is:

As we close behind a chapter of a ferociously-contested general election
behind us, we see not the end of a beginning, but the beginning of a
beautiful bipartisan cooperation in the next four years on the horizon.

The central task today under foreign policy is to reestablish Winterfell
as a military and economic superpower. Our leader should lead the world,
not apologize to the world; our economy should recover from the crisis,
not become the root of the crisis; our jobs need to attract the best
talents in the world to join us, not to run away from us; our military
needs to pre-empt strike, not to invite ambush. The murder of Eddard
Stark served as a sober wake-up call for us all.

So how can we achieve that? We need to do three things:

First, maintain strong military strength with sustained investment to
counter potential state-based threats, especially in the Western
Mooncific region.

Second, open Winterfell up to the global market by attracting more
high-skilled workers. Look at these talented GSB students in this room,
especially those international ones who have no idea what is
Winterfell's foreign policy but sit in anyway. Their brains so
intelligent, attitude so diligent, dance moves so elegant! Somebody
please give them visa so that they can stay here after graduation!

Last but not the least, adopt a stronger stance toward Casterly Rock's
unfair trade practice by imposing higher duties to level the play field
in a global market. Ensure they will not steal our intellectual
properties; ensure they will not manipulate their currency; and ensure
they will not grow at the expense of Winterfell. Fear not, the one
gazillion dollars we owe to the Lannisters; fear not, the so many gold
medals they won at the Hunger Games, because, fear leads to anger, anger
leads to hate, hates leads to suffering of Winterfell people!

You may say I'm a dreamer, but I'm not the only one. I hope by the end
of this debate you will join us. And the Starks and the Lannisters will
live as one, leading Winterfell to prosperity, once again!
