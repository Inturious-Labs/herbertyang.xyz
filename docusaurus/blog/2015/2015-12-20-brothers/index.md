Amusing moments of two brothers

![brothers](./img/brothers-5.jpg)

<!--truncate-->

松梢藏远山[^1]，晨辉跃阑干

窗外风渐歇，堂内琴正酣[^2]

凌客[^3]心力憔[^4]，黑白键气豪

湖畔[^5]问天下，浥尘[^6]志更坚

![brother 1](./img/brothers-1.jpg)

![brother 2](./img/brothers-2.jpg)

My friend [LYZ](http://weibo.com/u/1743289671) has responded with a much better one, as usual:

凌客远来烽烟倦

瓜舍[^7]湖畔暂偷闲

松影琴声浮云淡

浥尘一曲且悠然
