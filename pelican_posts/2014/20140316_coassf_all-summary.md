structure of this blog was meant to force readers to read every article.

This series is not finished yet. Despite of the various works I've done
during the Sloan year, I'm yet to unveil my most satisfactory and
ambitious project for my distinguished Sloan Class of 2013. Tentatively
I'll debut that on our ten year anniversary dinner and dance.
