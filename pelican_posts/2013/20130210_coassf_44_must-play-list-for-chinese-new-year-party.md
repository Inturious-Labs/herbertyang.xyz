制作人：*[桂子山人](http://www.xiami.com/u/1301213)*

For the Stanford GSB Sloan Fellow Chinese New Year Party on this Friday,
Feb 08，2013. The songs are picked for:

1. Their festive and party spirit;
2. Their iconic （many of them legendary） status in the Chinese music industry;
3. Their ability to represent certain distinctive genres of Chinese music;
4. Their coverage of the key Chinese regions such as Hong Kong, Taiwan,
    Southeast Asia, and Mainland. The audience for this party consists
    of 80 Sloan Fellows with their partners/children that come from 30+
    countries all over the world with age ranging from 30 - 45
    （roughly）.

Does it represent my taste? Of course not, but it's a great list for a
party like Chinese New Year.
